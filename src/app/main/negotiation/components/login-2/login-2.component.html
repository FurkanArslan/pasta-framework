Welcome to Neşter Negotiation Simulator
We address the problem of designing a multi-stakeholder socio-technical system (STS). Stake-holder requirements often conflict with each other,which makes STS design cumbersome for humans. To enable automation and assist humans with the design process, we propose an agent-based negotiation framework, NEŞTER, where each agent acts on behalf of a stakeholder and negotiates with other agents on the STS specification by generating bids consisting of norms. We represent stakeholder requirements as values for agents and take a value-based approach to generate offers based on a re-vision of the norms in the bid of the opponent, so that it contributes towards the agent’s value preferences. We evaluate NEŞTER using a health care privacy scenario, where an automated agent negotiates with a human counterpart.
